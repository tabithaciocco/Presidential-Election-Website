Candidates

The candidates for this year's election are Hillary Clinton and Donald trump


Hillary Clinton

Hillary Clinton was born on October 26, 1947, in Chicago, Illinois. In 1975, she married Bill Clinton. When Bill became president, she served as first lady from 1993 to 2001. After serving as a U.S. senator from 2001 to 2009, Clinton announced that she wanted to run for president. In the primaries, Hillary lost to Barack Obama, who later appointed her as Secretary of State and a part of his cabinet. In 2015, she announced that she wanted to run for president again. In 2016, she became the first woman in United States history to be nominated as a potential president.

Donald Trump

Donald Trump was born in 1946, in Queens, New York. Keeping to his roots, Trump stayed in Manhattan, New York, and became involved in large building projects. In 1980, Donald Trump opened the Grand Hyatt. This made him into one of the most known developers in Manhattan. It wasn't until 2015 that Donald Trump announced that he would be running for the republican party as the potential presidential nominee. On July 19, 2016, Donald Trump became the official Republican canidate.
