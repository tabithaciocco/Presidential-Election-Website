Candidates

The candidates for this year's election are Hillary Clinton and Donald trump


Hillary Clinton

Hillary Clinton was born on October 26, 1947, in Chicago, Illinois. In 1975, she married Bill Clinton. They both had degrees in law. When Bill Clinton became president, she served as first lady from 1993 to 2001. After serving as a U.S. senator from 2001 to 2009, Clinton announced that she wanted to run for president. During the 2008 Democratic primaries, she lost to Barack Obama, who later appointed her as secretary of state and a part of the cabinet. In the spring of 2015, she announced that she wanted to run for president again. In 2016, she became Clinton became the first woman in United States history to be nominated as a potential president

Donald Trump

Donald Trump was born in 1946, in Queens, New York. Keeping to his roots, Trump stayed in Manhattan, New York, and became involved in large building projects. In 1980, Donald Trump opened the Grand Hyatt. This made him into one of the most known developers in Manhattan. In 2015, Trump turned his attention to politics, and announced that he would be running for the republican party as the presidential nominee. After winning the primary, Trump became the official Republican candidate.
