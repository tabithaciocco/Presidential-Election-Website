The Different Parties

The TWO major parties are Democratic and Republican.

Democratic Party

The Democratic Party is one of the two major political parties in the United States of America. This party can be traced back to Thomas Jefferson and James Madison's Democratic-Republican Party. However, the modern-day Democratic Party wasn't founded until about 1828. The democratic party is the world's oldest active political party. Hillary Clinton is the Democratic Party's current nominee for the 2016 election.

Republican Party

The Republican Party, or GOP is the second of the two major political parties in the United States of America. There have been 18 Republican presidents, the first being Abraham Lincoln, who served from 1861 to 1865. Donald Trump is the Republican Party's current nominee for the 2016 election.
